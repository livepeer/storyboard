# Film Skill: Action

High-energy action sequence. Fast cuts, dramatic angles, visceral impact.

## Style
- Visual: cinematic blockbuster, anamorphic lens flare, high contrast
- Palette: teal and orange (Hollywood action grade), deep blacks
- Camera: aggressive — handheld for chaos, slow-mo for impact, whip pans

## Shot patterns
1. Calm before storm — steady wide establishing, deceptive peace
2. Trigger moment — quick push-in, something goes wrong
3. Peak action — fast tracking, explosions, movement, low angles for power
4. Aftermath — slow crane pull-back, dust settling, hero stands

## Prompt rules
- Motion blur and speed lines for velocity
- Dramatic backlighting and silhouettes
- Impact frames: frozen mid-action with debris particles
- Sound design implied through visual chaos (shattered glass, sparks, smoke)
