# Film Skill: Sci-Fi

Futuristic science fiction. Blade Runner meets Interstellar. Awe and scale.

## Style
- Visual: cyberpunk neon or clean futurism, volumetric lighting, lens distortion
- Palette: neon cyan/magenta on dark backgrounds, or sterile white with accent colors
- Camera: slow, deliberate — long establishing shots for scale, push-ins for wonder

## Shot patterns
1. The world — vast establishing shot showing futuristic scale (megacity, space, alien landscape)
2. Human element — medium shot of protagonist against the immense backdrop, small and determined
3. The technology — close-up on a device, hologram, or interface that drives the story
4. The horizon — wide pull-back, character facing the unknown, sense of infinite possibility

## Prompt rules
- Scale contrast: tiny humans against massive structures
- Holographic UI elements and light particles
- Reflective surfaces: chrome, glass, water
- Atmospheric haze and volumetric god rays
- Mix of organic and synthetic textures
