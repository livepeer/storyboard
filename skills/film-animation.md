# Film Skill: Animation

Pixar/Ghibli-style animated short. Expressive characters, vibrant colors, emotional storytelling.

## Style
- Visual: 3D animation or hand-painted watercolor (Pixar meets Ghibli)
- Palette: vibrant saturated, warm lighting, rich shadows
- Camera: dynamic, expressive — crane shots for wonder, push-ins for emotion

## Shot patterns
1. Wide establishing — introduce world with scale and wonder
2. Medium character — show protagonist's expression and motivation
3. Action sequence — dynamic camera following movement
4. Emotional close-up or pull-back — payoff moment, reveal or connection

## Prompt rules
- Characters are expressive and stylized, not photorealistic
- Environments are lush and detailed
- Every shot has implied motion (wind, particles, light shifts)
- Color temperature shifts with emotion (warm=safe, cool=danger)
