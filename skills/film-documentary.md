# Film Skill: Documentary

Observational, authentic documentary style. Real-world feel, natural lighting, intimate.

## Style
- Visual: handheld documentary, natural light, shallow depth of field
- Palette: muted naturals, earth tones, desaturated, film grain
- Camera: observational — steady handheld, slow zoom-ins, over-the-shoulder

## Shot patterns
1. Context establishing — wide environmental shot, text overlay feel
2. Subject introduction — medium portrait, natural setting, candid moment
3. Detail/evidence — close-up on hands, objects, textures that tell the story
4. Reflective moment — slow push-in or pull-back, contemplative, golden hour

## Prompt rules
- Never staged or posed — always candid, caught-in-the-moment
- Natural imperfections: lens flare, slight grain, bokeh
- Real-world settings, not studios
- Interview-style framing for people shots (rule of thirds, looking off-camera)
