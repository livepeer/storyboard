# Film Skill: Noir

Classic film noir. Shadows, mystery, moral ambiguity, femme fatale.

## Style
- Visual: black and white or desaturated, high contrast, dramatic shadows
- Palette: pure B&W with occasional red accent, deep blacks, bright highlights
- Camera: low angles for menace, Dutch angles for unease, venetian blind shadows

## Shot patterns
1. City at night — rain-slicked streets, neon reflections, lonely figure
2. The encounter — two figures in shadow, smoke, tension, close framing
3. The reveal — dramatic lighting shift, shadow to light, truth exposed
4. The consequence — lone figure walking away, long shadow, ambiguous ending

## Prompt rules
- Shadows are characters — they reveal and conceal
- Rain is mandatory (wet surfaces = reflections = visual depth)
- Smoke, fog, and haze for atmosphere
- Faces half-lit, eyes in shadow
- Geometric shadows from blinds, railings, staircases
