# Studio Ghibli Style Pack

When active, all image and video prompts are wrapped with Ghibli aesthetics automatically.

## Best Practices
- Works best with nature scenes, character portraits, and fantasy settings
- Lean into illustrated look — avoid photorealistic subjects
- For video: keep motion gentle, avoid fast cuts
- Landscapes benefit from detailed background art with layered depth
- Characters should have simple expressive faces with large eyes

## Prompt Tips
- Add "wide establishing shot" for landscape scenes
- Use "close-up portrait" for character focus
- Include weather/atmosphere: "misty morning", "golden sunset", "rainy day"
- Nature elements: "wildflowers", "ancient trees", "flowing stream"

## What Gets Injected
- Prefix: "studio ghibli style, hand-painted watercolor, soft pastel colors, whimsical, Miyazaki inspired, "
- Suffix: ", ghibli aesthetic, warm gentle lighting, detailed background art"
- Video: "+ gentle camera movement, dreamy animation, ghibli film quality"
