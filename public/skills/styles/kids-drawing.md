# Kid's Drawing Style Pack

When active, all image and video prompts use a childlike crayon drawing aesthetic.

## Best Practices
- Simple subjects work best — animals, houses, people, sun, trees
- Avoid complex scenes with many details
- Bold primary colors: red, blue, yellow, green
- Stick figures and simple shapes are on-brand
- For video: bouncy, playful motion

## Prompt Tips
- "a happy dog" → crayon dog with big smile
- "a house with a garden" → simple house with triangle roof, flowers
- Keep prompts short and concrete — kids draw what they see, not abstract concepts

## What Gets Injected
- Prefix: "children's crayon drawing style, simple colorful shapes, thick wobbly lines, naive art, "
- Suffix: ", kid's artwork, construction paper texture, bright primary colors"
- Video: "+ playful animation, bouncy movement, cartoon style"
